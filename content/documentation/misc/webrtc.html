--- title: WebRTC --- h1. WebRTC <%= link_to "WebRTC", "http://www.webrtc.org" %> enables Real-Time Communications (RTC) audio/video capabilities in Web browsers and other devices such as smartphones. WebRTC protocol specifications are being developed by the <%= link_to "IETF Rtcweb", "http://tools.ietf.org/wg/rtcweb" %> workgroup. <%= link_to "Client-side APIs", "http://www.w3.org/TR/webrtc/" %> are being defined by the <%= link_to "W3C WebRTC", "http://www.w3.org/2011/04/webrtc-charter.html" %> workgroup.
